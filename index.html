Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need To Know

Still have questions? Here are the most common ones we receive from businesses looking to automate their customer communication.

Why is it called ConceirgeOS?

ConciergeOS is inspired by the idea of a concierge, a trusted assistant who helps people quickly and efficiently. Our platform brings that same experience to businesses through AI, providing intelligent voice and chat assistants that answer customer questions, book appointments, automate workflows, and support customers 24/7.

How does the AI Receptionist work?

It answers calls and chats, qualifies leads, books appointments, and integrates with your existing business tools.

Can I try the AI before booking?

Yes. Test our AI chat on this page anytime, no signup required.

Can the AI answer phone calls?

Yes. It answers calls 24/7, transfers callers, takes messages, and books appointments.

Does it work with my CRM?

Yes. It integrates with HubSpot, Salesforce, Google Calendar, Outlook, n8n, Twilio, and more.

Which businesses can use ConciergeOS?

Perfect for healthcare, legal, real estate, home services, automotive, hospitality, and more.

How long does setup take?

Most businesses are ready within a few days, depending on integrations and customization.

Can the AI speak multiple languages?

Yes. ConciergeOS can be configured to support multiple languages for voice and chat experiences.

Can it answer after business hours?

Yes. Your AI receptionist stays available 24/7 so customers can still get help when your team is offline.

Can it transfer callers to my staff?

Yes. It can route qualified callers, urgent enquiries, or specific requests to the right team member.

Can it work with WhatsApp?

Yes. ConciergeOS can be connected to WhatsApp alongside voice, chat, calendar, and CRM workflows.

Can I customize it for my business?

Absolutely. We tailor the receptionist, automations, integrations, and conversation flows to match your business.
